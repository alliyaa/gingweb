Home Features Gingy

On the homepage of home, if you're on following, you can see videos, photos, and outfit votings from creators you follow. Below the post you can see where the outfit is from and an image link to the outfit. You can like, comment, share and repost videos

On the homepage of home, if you're on groups, you can see the groups you are a part of and their posts. If you click on a specific post you can like, comment, share or repost it

On the homepage of home, if you're on your posts, you can create a new post, see your outfit voting posts, your photo posts, and your video posts. In all 3 pages of home you can search as well

If you click on the outfit voting posts from screen 3 , you'll be able to see all your outfit voting posts and if you click on a specific outfit, you can see the results. If you click on the black rectangle you can see who voted for the first top, and if you click on the grey rectangle you can see who voted for the second top. You can also see the likes, comments, shares, reposts and can delete the post as well
